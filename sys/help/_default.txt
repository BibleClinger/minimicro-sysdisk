Welcome to Mini Micro!
Enter MiniScript code at the prompt.
For language help, see: `http://miniscript.org`

When typing a command, Mini Micro will sometimes make
suggestions in faint text after the cursor.  Just press 
the `Tab` key to accept the suggested text.

To break out of any program, press Control-C.
To clear the screen, enter `clear`

For more help, enter `help "topics"`
Remember to use quotation marks!
